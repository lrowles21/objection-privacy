1 Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Objection ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"), developed and published by Landon Rowles, handles information in connection with your use of the App on Apple iOS devices.

By downloading and using Objection, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App. This policy applies solely to the Objection iOS application and does not govern any third-party websites or services that may be linked to or referenced within the App.

Objection is a gamified legal education app. It is designed to teach players about legal concepts, courtroom procedures, and argumentation skills in an interactive format. It is not a law firm, does not provide legal advice, and does not establish any attorney-client relationship.

🔒
No Backend Server

Objection has no backend server, database, or cloud infrastructure. All game state, scores, and progress are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's UserDefaults API and are never transmitted externally by the App itself.

3 Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Objection is free to download and play. To support ongoing development, the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC. AdMob may display banner ads and interstitial (full-screen) ads during your use of the App.

When ads are displayed, Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device, which may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Apple's Identifier for Advertisers, or IDFA)
  • IP address and general approximate location derived from IP
  • Device make, model, and operating system version
  • App interaction events related to ad delivery
  • Ad click and impression data
  • Diagnostic and performance information related to ad rendering

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policies and terms of service, not by Objection or Landon Rowles. We do not receive, access, or store any of the data Google collects for advertising purposes.

ℹ
Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14 and later, Apple requires apps to request your permission before accessing your device's IDFA for personalized advertising. If you decline tracking when prompted, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads. Either way, ads will still appear in the App.

You can learn more about how Google collects and uses data through AdMob by reviewing:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

To opt out of personalized advertising by Google across apps and websites, you can visit Google Ad Settings or adjust your iOS privacy settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

★ Game Center & Leaderboards

Objection integrates with Apple Game Center to provide achievements and competitive leaderboards. If you are signed into Game Center on your device, the App may submit your game data — including your scores, streak length, and achievement progress — to Apple's Game Center service.

Please be aware that these scores are uploaded to global leaderboards. This means your submitted scores, together with your Game Center nickname (alias) and avatar, may be publicly visible to other players who view the leaderboards or achievements. This is a core function of the competitive leaderboard feature.

Game Center is an Apple service. The handling of your Game Center identity and data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can control or disable Game Center at any time in your device Settings > Game Center, and you may choose not to sign in to Game Center, in which case no scores are uploaded.

4 Data Storage & Security

All data generated within Objection — including your progress, unlocked content, and in-app settings — is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server operated by us
  • Is subject to iOS system-level encryption and the security model of your device
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the App or erase your device
  • Is not backed up to iCloud unless you have enabled iCloud backup for your device overall (controlled by Apple, not by us)

Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our own infrastructure, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information through Objection is negligible. We nonetheless encourage you to keep your iOS device and operating system up to date as a general security best practice.

5 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any personal information about you with third parties, because we do not collect any personal information in the first place.

The only third party that may collect data in connection with your use of Objection is Google AdMob, as described in Section 3. That data is governed entirely by Google's privacy policies.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process (such as a court order or government request), but given that we store no personal user data, any such disclosure would not involve information about individual users.

6 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) imposes requirements on operators of websites and online services directed to children under the age of 13 in the United States.

Objection is a legal education app intended for a general audience of users aged 13 and older. The App is not knowingly directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

⚠
Note for Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child under the age of 13 has used Objection and you have concerns about data collected by Google AdMob, please review Google's policies on children's privacy and consider restricting ad tracking on your child's device via iOS Screen Time or Privacy settings. You may also contact us at the address below.

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe this has occurred, please contact us immediately.

7 Your Choices & Rights

Because Objection does not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your request. However, you retain the following controls:

  • Ad Tracking: You can limit ad tracking or opt out of personalized ads on your iOS device under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and by resetting or limiting your Advertising Identifier.
  • App Data: You can delete all locally stored App data by uninstalling Objection from your device.
  • Google Ad Personalization: Manage Google's use of your data for advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Residents of certain jurisdictions (such as California under the CCPA, or the European Economic Area under the GDPR) may have additional rights with respect to personal information. Because we do not collect personal information, most of these rights are satisfied by default. For advertising-related data collected by Google, please refer to Google's policies and your regional data protection authority.
